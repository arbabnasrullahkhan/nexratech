Capabilities

Everything you need to record professionally

A complete recording studio without the complexity. Built on WebRTC and Canvas APIs.

OBS-Grade Capture

Select entire screen, a browser tab, or a specific window with live preview before you record.

Webcam Overlay

Draggable, resizable Picture-in-Picture webcam with circle, square, and rounded shapes plus mirror mode.

Audio Mixing

Mix microphone and system audio with individual volume controls, noise suppression, and echo cancellation.

Live Annotations

Draw freehand, add directional arrows, highlight areas, and blur sensitive data — all while recording.

Canvas Burn Engine

Watermarks are invisble during recording and permanently burned frame-by-frame into the final export.

PWA — Works Offline

Install as a desktop app. Record and annotate even without internet. Sync history when back online.
